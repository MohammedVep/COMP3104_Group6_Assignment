This course is designed to help you focus on managing dev operations and deployment 
of development activities. Dev Operations are used in may ways to help reduce IT 
overhead and get products delivered to customers on time, on schedule and on budget. 
Dev ops also improve communication and collaboration across teams and they are used 
to remove manual tasks both of mundane or complex nature. Devops allow automated 
deployment of many web applications. In order to drive innovation in connected 
society quickly and effectively when people do not like to go anywhere and not 
everyone is interested in hanging out, dev ops has been more important than ever 
before.
